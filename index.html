Our Manifesto

We believe that presence is the rarest gift.
That in a time of endless scroll and curated feeds, being witnessed — truly seen — is healing.

Witness is a digital campfire. It’s where two people, strangers or not, can meet in real time — on video — and offer one another their full, undivided presence.

There are no likes. No comments. No filters. Just human beings, slowing down, and holding space for one another.
